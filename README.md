## Requirements for AppleCatch

## AppleCatch Rules
- Use your phone in landscape mode and tilt it left or right to move the basket accordingly
- catch as many apples as possible to get a high score
- try to avoid the bombs, these remove points from your score and remove one of your 3 lives
